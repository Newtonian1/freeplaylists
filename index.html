About

Freeplaylists is a personal passion project of mine. As a music lover and an avid Rocket League player, I wanted to create a repository of playlists to listen to while in freeplay. This site is meant to accomplish this and, hopefully, more in the future. I am looking forward to adding more functionality to this site as I grow as a developer. Thank you for visiting this site, and I hope you enjoy the music. Now go ball chase!
